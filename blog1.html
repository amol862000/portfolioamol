The Art of Responsive Web Design
Responsive web design is an essential approach in modern web development, ensuring a seamless user experience across a myriad of devices. The core principles involve using flexible grids, fluid layouts, and media queries, allowing websites to adapt dynamically to different screen sizes.
Media Queries: Employ media queries to apply specific styles based on the characteristics of the device, such as screen width, height, or orientation. This adaptability is crucial for delivering an optimal experience on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
